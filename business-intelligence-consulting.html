Business Intelligence Solutions We Create

Enterprise intelligence

  • 360-degree company view via integration and analysis of resource, process, financial, customer, marketing, sales, etc., data.
  • Company’s performance analytics.
  • Strategic corporate planning.
  • Prediction and forecasting (financial forecasting, demand forecasting, etc.)

Operational intelligence

  • Business processes monitoring and analysis.
  • Detection of deviations and undesirable patterns.
  • Cause-effect analysis, bottleneck recognition.
  • Operational performance prediction and forecasting.

Financial intelligence

  • Continuous visibility into corporate financial performance.
  • Tracking a company’s revenue, expenses and profitability.
  • Financial planning and budgeting.
  • Financial risk forecasting and management.

Customer intelligence

  • Customer segmentation and customer behavior analytics.
  • Customer value analytics.
  • Customer satisfaction analysis.
  • Customer behavior predictive modeling, churn risk management.

Brand and product intelligence

  • Product/service profitability analysis and portfolio optimization.
  • Analysis of marketing campaigns and loyalty programs efficiency.
  • Brand portfolio and brand awareness analysis.
  • Competitor benchmarking.

Human resources intelligence

  • Employee/department performance analysis.
  • Employee experience and satisfaction analysis.
  • Identification of the employee retention strategy effectiveness.
  • Employee hiring strategy analysis and optimization.

Manufacturing intelligence

  • Overall equipment effectiveness, analysis and optimization.
  • Manufacturing process quality monitoring and management.
  • Equipment maintenance scheduling.

Supply chain intelligence

  • Supply chain visibility and monitoring.
  • Identifying demand drivers, consumer demand forecasting.
  • Demand/supply balancing.
  • Supply chain risks analytics.

Transportation and logistics intelligence

  • Operational capacity planning and optimization.
  • Predictive analytics for vehicle maintenance (failure prediction, recommendation of maintenance actions, etc.).
  • Vehicle supply allocation and vehicle dispatch management.
  • IoT data analytics for safe cargo delivery (data on cargo temperature, humidity, etc.; data on driver behavior, vehicle condition, etc.).

Healthcare intelligence

  • Patient health condition monitoring, patient treatment optimization.
  • Assessment of patient risks and personalized care plan recommendations.
  • Fraud detection in healthcare insurance.
  • Medical staff workload prediction and work shifts optimization.
  • Optimization of clinical space and equipment usage.
